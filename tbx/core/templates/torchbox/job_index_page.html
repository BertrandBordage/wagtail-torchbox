6 killer reasons to join Torchbox

  • 01

    Work with this lot

    Work with our brilliant and talented team of designers, developers, project managers, and digital marketers. We’re a lovely bunch of sports enthusiasts, creatives, musicians, cyclists, readers, cooks and more.

  • 02

    Lovely clients

    We're very selective about who we work with. At the end of the day you'll go home knowing you've helped to make the world a better place.

  • 03

    Cool projects

    From large, complex website projects to campaign platforms and custom apps, we've always got lots of interesting creative and technical challenges to tackle.

  • 04

    Ski trips

    Occasionally we all head off together for a few days of fun in the snow, complete with ski chalet, hot tub and lots of food and drink.

  • 05

    Awesome office perks

    Our offices come equipped with superior coffee, tea, fresh fruit and bread. We’ve got standing desks and comfortable sofas. Make sure you try out the juicer.

  • 06

    Viral Christmas cards

    Most years we put together a collaborative company Christmas card - and sometimes they go viral! Past initiatives have included talking mooses, iPad dances, on-demand snow machines, creative map plotting and interactive choirs.
